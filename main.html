Holiday destination

  1. Eiffel Tower – A visit to Paris is incomplete without seeing the iconic Eiffel Tower.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa, this is the world's largest art museum.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – Admire the stunning Gothic architecture of this historic cathedral.
  4. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – Located at the highest point in the city, this basilica offers breathtaking views of Paris.
  5. Champs-Élysées and Arc de Triomphe – Stroll down one of the most famous avenues and visit the iconic arch.
  • Baguette – A crispy French staple that's perfect for breakfast or lunch.
  • Escargots – Try the famous French snails cooked in garlic and butter.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes that can be sweet or savory, a must-try street food.
  • Ratatouille – A flavorful vegetable stew, perfect for vegetarians.
  • Macarons – Delightful sweet treats that come in a variety of flavors.
